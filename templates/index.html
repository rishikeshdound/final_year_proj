About Team

We are a group of final-year students from AISSMS IOIT, passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. Our project, a predictive maintenance Flask website for fault diagnosis in three-phase Voltage Source Inverters (VSI), is the culmination of our academic journey. Guided by our esteemed mentor DR.R.B.Dhumale, we have developed a solution that utilizes advanced algorithms to predict potential faults, ensuring timely maintenance and minimizing downtime. Through this project, we aim to contribute to the field of industrial automation and enhance operational efficiency.

We have developed a predictive maintenance Flask website designed for fault diagnosis in three-phase Voltage Source Inverters (VSI). This platform leverages advanced algorithms to predict potential faults, ensuring timely maintenance and minimizing downtime. With an intuitive interface and real-time analytics, it empowers users to maintain optimal operational efficiency in industrial applications.
